# MedAssur — Design Brainstorm

## Selected Approach: B — "Quiet Luxury Clinic"

### Design Movement
Editorial Quiet Luxury — the visual language of Kinfolk magazine meets a Park Avenue private practice. Warmth without sentimentality. Authority without sterility.

### Core Principles
1. **Restraint over decoration** — every element earns its place; nothing decorative for decoration's sake
2. **Warmth signals trust** — warm neutrals and organic textures replace clinical white and cold blue
3. **Typography carries the weight** — headlines do the heavy lifting; imagery supports, never dominates
4. **Asymmetric breathing room** — layouts offset to the left or right, never perfectly centered grids

### Color Philosophy
- **Background:** Warm linen `oklch(0.97 0.012 80)` — not white, not cream, but the color of fine stationery
- **Foreground:** Deep charcoal `oklch(0.18 0.010 60)` — warm black, never cold
- **Primary / Signature Brand Color:** Forest green `oklch(0.38 0.085 155)` — the color of trust, nature, and quiet authority
- **Accent:** Aged bronze `oklch(0.62 0.085 65)` — warmth, prestige, calls-to-action
- **Muted surfaces:** `oklch(0.93 0.015 80)` — for cards, dividers, subtle backgrounds

### Typography System
- **Display / Headlines:** `Cormorant Garamond` — elegant, editorial, high contrast serif
- **Body / UI:** `DM Sans` — clean, modern, humanist sans

### Brand Essence
**MedAssur** — the private physician relationship that used to require knowing the right people. *Discreet. Capable. Always there.*

Personality adjectives: **Authoritative. Warm. Unwavering.**

### Brand Voice
- Example headline: *"The doctor you'd call at midnight. Now you actually can."*
- Example CTA: *"Secure your membership"*
- Ban: "Welcome to our website", "Get started today", "Revolutionary healthcare"

---

## Style Decisions
- Nav transitions from transparent to `oklch(0.97 0.012 80)/96%` with backdrop blur on scroll
- Section labels use `DM Sans` small-caps, `oklch(0.62 0.085 65)` bronze color, letter-spacing 0.12em
- All CTA buttons use forest green background with linen text, bronze hover glow
- Pull-quote borders use 3px solid forest green left border
- Hero uses warm linen background (NOT dark) — first impression is warm private practice
- Dark cinematic panels are contrast moments only, never the primary expression
- The dominant first impression must be warm linen and forest green
- Imagery depicts discreet private medical relationship through warm office details, consultation materials, physician craft, and natural light
- MedAssur's identity must be recognizable within the first viewport: serif "M" mark and forest green are brand anchors
- Services use editorial list with oversized serif numerals, not uniform card grids
- How It Works uses full-width editorial 3-column layout per step
- Asymmetric 60/40 or 11-column grid splits throughout
